About ParrotNerds

ParrotNerds is an innovative edtech platform focused on delivering high-impact learning in Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, and STEM education. Designed for today’s curious and future-focused students, our programs provide the tools, knowledge, and confidence they need to excel in a rapidly evolving digital world.

Our curriculum integrates AI, machine learning, robotics, and coding with real-world applications—ensuring students learn through hands-on projects, simulations, and interactive experiences.

Beyond technology, we nurture emotionally aware, balanced individuals who are prepared for life, not just work.

What We Offer

Robotics & Automation

Explore mechanical systems, control logic, and intelligent machines. Build and program functional robots using sensors, microcontrollers, and automation workflows.

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Understand algorithms, machine learning, and ethical tech practices. Create simple AI applications while developing critical thinking and innovation skills.

STEM & Coding Courses

From beginner coding to app development and game design—our courses blend problem-solving, logic, and creativity to build tech-savvy innovators.

Mental Wellness Integration

Mindfulness, emotional resilience, and confidence-building activities are embedded in every course to support holistic development and well-being.

In today’s rapidly evolving world, understanding Artificial Intelligence and Robotics is no longer a luxury—it’s a necessity. As technology becomes an integral part of every industry, early exposure to AI and robotics can empower both children and adults to stay ahead of the curve.
