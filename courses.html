Our Courses at ParrotNerds

Artificial Intelligence

The Artificial Intelligence (AI) course at ParrotNerds offers an immersive dive into the world of intelligent systems, equipping students with the knowledge to understand and create their own AI models. Students begin by learning the foundational principles of AI, including machine learning, neural networks, deep learning, and natural language processing. They explore how AI impacts industries like healthcare, finance, entertainment, and automotive, and apply their knowledge through hands-on projects.

Robotics & Machinery

This course ignites curiosity and builds practical skills in robotics, automation, and machine design. Students learn mechanical design, kinematics, sensors, and robot programming to perform tasks ranging from simple movements to complex actions.

Information Technology (IT)

Students explore computer systems, networking, programming, web development, and cybersecurity. They learn programming languages like Python, Java, and C++, and build websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and backend tech.

Public Speaking

Students build confidence and master the art of engaging audiences. The course covers overcoming stage fright, organizing speeches, vocal variety, body language, and tailoring speeches for different audiences.

Creative Writing

Students explore storytelling through fiction, non-fiction, poetry, and drama. They learn character development, plot structuring, dialogue creation, and vivid descriptions while refining drafts into polished pieces.

Phonics & Abacus

Young learners build literacy and math skills. Phonics teaches letter-sound relationships to improve reading fluency and pronunciation. Fun lessons help recognize letter sounds and blend them into words.

Mathematics & Vedic Math

Students build strong foundations in algebra, geometry, calculus, and statistics. Vedic Math techniques teach rapid mental calculations, simplifying algebra, and creative problem-solving.
